Special Prayers

🕌

Jummah Prayer

Khutbah: 1:00 PM

Prayer: 1:45 PM

Jummah prayer is held every Friday. Please arrive early to secure a spot. Separate facilities available for men and women.

🌙

Taraweeh Prayer

Time: After Isha Prayer

Duration: Approximately 1.5 hours

Special Taraweeh prayers are held during the blessed month of Ramadan. Join us for complete Quran recitation.

Prayer Guidelines

🧼

Purity

Ensure you are in a state of wudu before entering the prayer hall.

👕

Dress Code

Wear modest, clean clothing that covers the awrah.

🤫

Silence

Maintain silence in the prayer hall. Turn off mobile phones.

👞

Footwear

Remove shoes before entering the prayer area.

🧒

Children

Supervise children at all times. Designated family areas available.

🧴

Fragrance

Avoid strong fragrances out of consideration for others.
