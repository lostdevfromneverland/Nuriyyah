Islamic Education

📖

Quran Classes

We offer comprehensive Quran classes for all ages, from beginners to advanced learners. Our qualified teachers provide instruction in:

  • Quran recitation (Tajweed)
  • Quran memorization (Hifz)
  • Quran translation and Tafsir
  • Arabic language fundamentals

Schedule: Saturday & Sunday, 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM

🎓

Islamic Studies Program

Our Islamic Studies program covers essential topics for Muslims seeking to deepen their understanding of the faith:

  • Aqeedah (Islamic Creed)
  • Fiqh (Islamic Jurisprudence)
  • Seerah (Life of Prophet Muhammad)
  • Hadith Studies
  • Islamic History

Schedule: Tuesday & Thursday, 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM

👶

Children's Madrasah

Our children's program provides a fun and engaging environment for young Muslims to learn about their faith:

  • Basic Quran reading
  • Islamic manners and ethics
  • Stories of the Prophets
  • Islamic arts and crafts

Ages: 5-12 years | Schedule: Saturday, 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM

Funeral Services (Janazah)

We provide dignified funeral services for our community:

  • Ghusl (ritual washing)
  • Kafan (shrouding)
  • Janazah prayer
  • Burial arrangement assistance

Available: 24/7 | Emergency Contact

Confidential counseling services are available for:

  • Marital and family issues
  • Youth guidance
  • Spiritual guidance
  • Stress and anxiety support

  • Emergency food assistance

Distribution: Every Saturday, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
